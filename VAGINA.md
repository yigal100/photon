## VAGINA: Voice And Governance In Natural Argument

Welcome to the Iron project! Unlike traditional Codes of Conduct, the VAGINA manifesto aims to foster an open, irreverent, and brutally honest environment. While we encourage creativity and innovation, we also uphold a few key principles to ensure this remains a haven for rigorous debate, free expression, and (most importantly) productive discussions about programing language design & implementation.

### Principles of V.A.G.I.N.A.

### **V**: **Veracity Over Sensitivity**
- "Facts don't care about your feelings." In this space, truth and reason reign supreme. Engage with honesty and intellectual rigor, even if the truth stings a little.

### **A**: **Arguments, Not Agendas**
- This project is for discussing **programing language design**. Political debates, social crusades, and unrelated controversies should be taken elsewhere. Keep the focus sharp and relevant.

### **G**: **Good-Natured Disagreement**
- Disagreement is the spice of discourse. Respect opposing viewpoints and counter them with logic, wit, and evidence—not personal attacks.

### **I**: **Ideas Over Identity**
- Contributions are judged by the strength of the ideas, not the identity or background of the contributor. Meritocracy > tribalism.

### **N**: **No Censorship, No Coddling**
- Freedom of expression is paramount. We don’t shy away from hard truths or difficult conversations. Bring your arguments, not your demands for safe spaces.

### **A**: **Always Stay On Topic**
- We’re here to create, critique, and refine ideas about **programing language design**. Stay focused. If it doesn’t involve language design, it doesn’t belong here.

---
### A Note on Humor
We encourage the use of wit, puns, and clever banter to keep the vibe light and fun. Just don’t let your jokes become the conversation’s focus. We're here to laugh, but we're also here to build something meaningful.

### Final Words
By adhering to the V.A.G.I.N.A principles, we aim to create a space where free expression thrives, intellectual debate flourishes, and meaningful contributions are celebrated.  

Let’s keep this project grounded in logic, reason, and fun. Follow these principles, and we’ll all get along swimmingly.
